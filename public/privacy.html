Privacy Policy for CastLink

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

CastLink is an independent app created and operated by a single developer.

CastLink lets users search connections between actors, movies, and TV shows, including common titles between actors, common actors between titles, and Kevin Bacon-style connection paths.

Information We Collect

CastLink does not require users to create an account or sign in.

CastLink does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

CastLink does not use advertising, third-party trackers, or third-party analytics SDKs.

Search Activity

When you use CastLink, you may enter searches for actors, movies, or TV shows. These searches are used only to provide the app’s functionality and display results.

CastLink does not maintain user accounts and does not intentionally store your searches on its own servers.

Shareable Links

CastLink can generate a shareable link for a search result. If you choose to share that link, the recipient may be able to view the same search result or connection represented by that link.

You should only share links if you are comfortable sharing those results with others.

Third-Party Data Provider

CastLink uses TMDB (The Movie Database) API to retrieve movie, TV, and person information.

Required TMDB Notice:
This product uses TMDB and the TMDB APIs but is not endorsed, certified, or otherwise approved by TMDB.

Because CastLink requests data from TMDB over the internet, TMDB may receive standard technical request information needed to return results.

Advertising and Tracking

CastLink does not use ads.

CastLink does not use advertising identifiers for tracking.

CastLink does not use third-party tracking technologies for profiling or targeted advertising.

Data Retention

CastLink does not intentionally retain personal information because it does not provide accounts or directly collect personal user data.

If any app state or preferences are stored locally on your device, that information remains on your device unless you choose to share it.

Children’s Privacy

CastLink is not directed to children under 13, and CastLink does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

CastLink uses reasonable measures to protect the app and its communications, but no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If it is updated, the Effective Date above will also be updated.
